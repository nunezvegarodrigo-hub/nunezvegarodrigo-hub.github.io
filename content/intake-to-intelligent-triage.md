---
title: Moving Beyond Intake to Intelligent Triage
tags:
  - legal-ops
---

Most legal teams have an intake problem. Requests come from everywhere. Email. Slack. Zoom chats. Hallway conversations. Spreadsheets. No standardization. No tracking. No prioritization.

Intake is not the goal. Intelligent triage is. The goal is not just to capture requests. It is to route them intelligently.

- **By complexity.** A simple NDA goes to junior or automation. A complex M&A goes to senior counsel. Match work to skill level.
- **By risk.** Low-risk marketing review gets faster turnaround. A high-risk litigation hold gets immediate attention. Match urgency to exposure.
- **By type.** Vendor contracts follow one workflow. Employment matters follow another. Customer agreements follow a third. Match process to pattern.
- **By business priority.** Deals that impact revenue this quarter move faster. Internal policies can wait. Match speed to value.

Intelligent triage means you stop treating every request the same. Because they are not. The goal is not a full inbox. The goal is the right work on the right desk at the right time.
