Most legal dashboards are ignored. Why? Because they talk about legal things. Leadership cares about business things. Your dashboard is not for you. It is for your audience.

If you want leadership to read it, speak their language.

- **Speed.** Average contract turnaround time. Time from request to signature. Days lost in review. Business understands speed.
- **Cost.** Hours spent per contract. External spend by matter type. Cost per deal closed. Business understands cost.
- **Risk.** Escalation rates. Deviation frequency. Compliance exceptions. Business understands risk.
- **Capacity.** Open requests. Work in progress. Team utilization. Business understands throughput.

One dashboard. Four categories. No legal jargon.

When you bring business metrics to the table, Legal Tech becomes a business discussion, not a technology debate.
