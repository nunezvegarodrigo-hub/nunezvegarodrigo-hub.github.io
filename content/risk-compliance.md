Legal risk and compliance are stuck in a once-a-year mindset. Someone reviews processes, checks boxes, and hopes nothing slipped through. By the time a problem surfaces, it is already a problem.

The shift is from periodic to continuous, from vague labels to real numbers, from hoping to knowing. Control does not come from locking everything down and saying no. It comes from designing visibility in.

This is the same principle that runs through [[invisible-automation|Invisible Automation]] and through the argument that [[blocking-ai-is-strategic-negligence|blocking AI is not safety]]. Real control is built, measured, and continuous. It is not declared once a year and forgotten.
