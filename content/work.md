I do not just write about this. I build it, and I measure it.

## Frameworks and models

- **[[invisible-automation|Invisible Automation]].** A model that captures and structures contract data directly from the tools lawyers already use, Word, email, and shared drives. It removes the adoption barrier that makes traditional CLM rollouts fail.
- **The Contract Intelligence Framework.** A reusable, configurable engine, capture then analyze then structure, that standardizes AI-driven metadata extraction across messy, non-standard contracts.
- **Operational analytics.** KPI dashboards and performance platforms that replace fragmented, Excel-based reporting with a single trusted view. See [[data-and-decisions|Data and Decision-Making]].

## Results that moved the number

- Scaled a strategic account from 30K to over 1.2M dollars a year by redesigning intake, governance, and analytics reporting.
- Cut contract negotiation cycle time by 30 percent with AI-assisted clause deviation tracking and template optimization.
- Designed a complexity-based delivery model that moved the operation from staff augmentation to scalable managed services.
- Run 3,000 to 5,000 contracts a year across a team of 35 in three regions, on a multi-million dollar portfolio.
