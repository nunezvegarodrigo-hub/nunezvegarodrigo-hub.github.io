I am Rodrigo Nuñez. I lead legal and service delivery operations, and I build the systems that make them work.

For over fifteen years I have scaled service models in complex, regulated environments, and turned manual legal work into structured data. My thesis is simple. The technology should adapt to the people, not the other way around. The best system is the one they never notice.

## What I do now

I am a Director at UnitedLex, running global enterprise accounts across Healthcare, Telecom, and Pharmaceutical clients. I lead a team of 35 across Mexico, India, and Europe, deliver thousands of contracts a year, and own a multi-million dollar service portfolio.

My work sits where legal, operations, and data meet. I take fragmented, manual processes and turn them into measured, predictable systems.

## The through line

Every role has pointed the same way. From founding legal-tech companies to running global delivery, the question has always been the same. How do you make expert work faster, cheaper, and less painful, without asking the expert to change how they work? That question is what became [[invisible-automation|Invisible Automation]].

## How I think

I have always been an entrepreneur, and I always will be. That is not a job title. It is a way of seeing.

It means I think outside the box by default. I look at how things are done and I ask how they could be better, and easier. And when I see the answer, I do not wait for permission. I jump in and build it.

I have always trusted that technology is our best ally. Not a threat to manage, a tool to use. The same goes for data. Facts are the foundation of every decision worth making.

But I never forget the other half. Intuition. Dreams. The crazy ideas that look impossible until someone tries them. The future is not something you wait for. It is something you imagine first, and then you build it.

## Background

- Founder and CEO, Lex Digital (2010 to 2011). An early contract lifecycle management platform.
- Master's in Business Intelligence, UNIR Mexico.
- Bachelor's in Corporate and Business Law, UNAM, Mexico City.
