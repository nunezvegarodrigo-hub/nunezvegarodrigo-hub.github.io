What if you knew what legal requests were coming before they arrived? Not magic. Just data. Your historical data predicts your future.

Look back at the last 12 months. Patterns will emerge.

- **Which contract types generate the most friction?** NDAs? MSAs? Vendor agreements? That is where to focus standardization.
- **Which departments send the most requests?** Sales? Procurement? HR? That is where to build self-service tools.
- **Which clauses predict long negotiations?** Indemnification? Liability caps? IP ownership? That is where to train your team.
- **Which months have peak volume?** Quarter end? Fiscal close? That is where to plan capacity.

Once you see the patterns, you stop reacting. You start preparing. You build playbooks before the requests arrive. You train teams before peak season. You automate before the volume spikes.

Reactive teams respond to demand. Predictive teams shape it.
